Guess The Number

The game "Guess the Number" is a popular guessing game where one player thinks of a number and the other player tries to guess it within a certain range. Here are the instructions for the game:

The guesser continues to make guesses based on the feedback received, narrowing down the possible range of the secret number. The game continues until the guesser successfully guesses the secret number. The guesser wins the game when their guess matches the secret number chosen by the thinker.

Guess the word

The computer selects a word from a predetermined list of words. The chosen word will be the one the player needs to guess.

The game continues until the player successfully guesses the chosen word or decides to give up. If the player guesses the word correctly, they win the game. If the player gives up, the computer reveals the correct word.

Rock Paper scissors

Both players simultaneously choose one of three options: rock, paper, or scissors. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.

Compare the choices: If both players choose the same option, it's a tie. Otherwise, the player with the winning choice wins the round. Play multiple rounds to determine the overall winner.
